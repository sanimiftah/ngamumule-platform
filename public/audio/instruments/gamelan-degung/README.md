# Gamelan Degung Audio Samples

Traditional Sundanese royal court gamelan ensemble with distinctive metallic tones.

## About Gamelan Degung
- Bronze metallophone ensemble
- Originally for Sundanese royal courts
- Smaller than Javanese gamelan
- Creates refined, elegant musical textures

## Cultural Significance
Gamelan Degung represents the refined artistic traditions of Sundanese royal courts and continues to be central to formal cultural events.
